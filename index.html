About Us

Welcome to Pradhan Library, a cornerstone of knowledge and community engagement located at the heart of Trivedi Mandir, Jegdev Nagar Ara (Bhojpur). Since our establishment, Pradhan Library has been a beacon of learning, offering a vast collection of books, periodicals, and digital resources tailored to meet the intellectual and informational needs of our diverse community. At Pradhan Library, we believe in the power of knowledge and the importance of providing accessible learning opportunities for all ages. Our library is more than just a place to borrow books—it's a space where individuals come together to explore, interact, and imagine.


Our Mission

To empower individuals with comprehensive access to information and to promote a love of reading and lifelong learning in a welcoming environment.


Our Vision

To be a vital part of our community that supports educational growth and enhances the quality of life through services and programming.


Our collection

Pradhan Library houses an extensive range of materials, including:

  • Latest bestsellers and literary classics
  • Educational and research materials covering various disciplines
  • A rich selection of magazines, newspapers, and journals
  • Children’s section with books and multimedia for all age groups
  • Digital resources, providing eBooks, audiobooks, and access to specialized databases
